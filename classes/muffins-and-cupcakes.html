Muffins & Cupcakes

$ 80.00 $ 100.00
For a limited time only, our classes are 20% off!

Whether you're craving something sweet or savory, muffins and cupcakes are an excellent starting point for your baking journey. We'll guide you through the fundamental steps to get you started, from mastering the art of batter mixing to achieving that perfect golden-brown finish.

While we provide the essential techniques, the forms and fillings of your confections are entirely up to you! Unleash your creativity as you experiment with an array of delicious flavor combinations and decorate your pastries into unique creations limited only by your imagination.

With our expert feline instructor by your side, offering guidance and encouragement every step of the way, this class promises to be a fun and stress-free experience.

Class specifications

Get ready for a truly immersive baking experience! Our classes are paws-on from start to finish, allowing you to dive right into the world of baking alongside our expert feline instructors.

First the instructor will show you the steps and share tips on ingredients, substitions, and techniques. Then you will execute the steps yourself under the supervision of the instructor. You'll be able to ask questions and receive personalized support and assistance if you encounter any difficulties during the baking process.

With each baking session, you'll bake up to 3 different treats, choosing from a variety of flavor combinations to customize your creations. At the end of class, you will have the opportunity to taste each item.

Classes are 3 hours long and are limited to 15 participants.

Cancellation policy
If you need to cancel your class reservation, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance to receive a full refund. No-shows without prior notification and cancellations made less than 24 hours of the scheduled class will not be eligible for a refund, but we'll be happy to reschedule your class for a future date, subject to availability. Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.
