Baking classes

At Whisks & Whiskers, we believe that every cat deserves the opportunity to indulge in the art of baking. As a cat-owned business, we're proud to offer baking classes designed exclusively for our feline friends. We welcome cats of all ages and skill levels, from curious kittens to sophisticated seniors.

Led by our expert feline instructors, each class provides a paws-on experience, with step-by-step guidance on kneading, mixing, decorating, and, of course, taste-testing. Class list:

  • Kneading Kittens: beginner level, where aspiring bakers start their journey into the world of feline baking.
  • Feline Flourishes: intermediate level, for those who have grasped the basics and are ready to dive deeper into more challenging recipes and techniques.
  • Purrfected Paws: advanced level, where experienced bakers refine their techniques and experiment with sophisticated flavors.
  • Catisserie Maestros: expert level, for those who have demonstrated exceptional skill and creativity in feline baking, crafting gourmet treats that delight even the most discerning of cat palates.
