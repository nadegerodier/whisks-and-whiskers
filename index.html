About us

At Whisks & Whiskers, we believe that every cat deserves the opportunity to indulge in the art of baking. As a cat-owned business, we're proud to offer baking classes designed exclusively for our feline friends. We welcome cats of all ages and skill levels, from curious kittens to sophisticated seniors.

Led by our expert feline instructors, each class provides a paws-on experience, with step-by-step guidance on kneading, mixing, decorating, and, of course, taste-testing.
